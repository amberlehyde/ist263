Book Your Stay at Artista in Wimberely, Texas!

Artista is a beautiful property located on the banks of the Smith Creek, offering guests a tranquil oasis right in the heart of the Texas Hill Country. This property is the perfect place to relax in privacy with 4.8 acres to call your own. Walk around and enjoy the nearly 1,000 feet of water frontage featuring the creek, pond, seasonal swimming hole and multiple seasonal waterfalls! Designer accents and attention to detail are just a few of the things you will love about the interior of Artista. When you first enter the home you will notice the large living room with coffered ceilings and ample windows to enjoy the view of the great outdoors. The large, comfy couch is a perfect spot to snuggle up and read or watch the deer trot by! The dining room nearby, with vaulted ceilings and a beautiful, dark wood dining table which can seat 8, has French door access to the creek side covered terrace and the level lawn canopied by large Oak trees. This beautiful room is a lovely spot to serve your guests while on vacation! The updated kitchen is any chef’s dream, with new stainless steel appliances including a double oven, microwave, side-by-side refrigerator/freezer, four-burner gas stove-top, dishwasher and coffee pot. The large center island is perfect for preparing your favorite gourmet meal. The kitchen is fully stocked with pots, pans, dishes, utensils and all the appliances you or the chef of your group will need! The two lovely bedrooms in the home have comfortable, chic furnishings, walk-in closets and wide doors modified for wheelchair access. The master bedroom has a plush king sized bed and private en-suite bathroom with a lovely, glass sink design and made by the artist designer of this home. The second bedroom, located on the opposite side of the home for maximum privacy, is furnished with a plush queen sized bed and also has its own private en-suite bathroom. With 2 bedrooms and 2 baths, this home suits a couple’s weekend, friends’ getaway or a family vacation! Artista also has high speed internet access and a rainwater collection tank which produces delicious drinking water! This new ranch-style home, built in 2009, truly exemplifies the Native Green Designed Waterfront! The clear, cool waters of the gently flowing creek and the rustle of the brilliant Oak trees blowing in the breeze provide hours of serenity and calm as you spend much needed alone time with your loved one. Rest assured, a relaxing and rejuvenating vacation awaits you at Artista, soon to be your favorite spot to get away from it all.
